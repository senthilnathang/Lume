## Search Intent Breakdown

### Problem-Aware (45% of searches)
"I have a problem with my current CRM. Need alternatives."
- Keywords: "airtable alternative", "salesforce too expensive", "crm without per-user fees"
- Blog posts: lume-vs-airtable.md, self-hosted-vs-cloud-crm.md
- Conversion: High (they want to switch)

### Solution-Aware (35% of searches)
"I'm looking for a CRM solution. What are my options?"
- Keywords: "open source crm", "crm comparison", "crm software"
- Blog posts: open-source-crm-comparison.md, why-open-source-crm.md
- Conversion: Medium (still researching)

### Knowledge-Seeking (20% of searches)
"I want to understand CRM options and trends."
- Keywords: "crm trends 2026", "why open source", "crm best practices"
- Blog posts: why-open-source-crm.md, privacy-first-crm.md
- Conversion: Low (educational stage)

## SEO Implementation Checklist

- [ ] Keyword in H1 tags
- [ ] Keyword in meta title (primary keyword)
- [ ] Keyword in meta description
- [ ] Keyword in first 100 words
- [ ] 1-2 internal links from other posts
- [ ] Natural keyword density (1-2% of text)
- [ ] Semantic keywords included (synonyms, related terms)
- [ ] Long-tail keywords naturally included
- [ ] Images with alt text containing keywords
- [ ] Schema.org markup (BlogPosting schema)
- [ ] External links (authority sites)
- [ ] Readability score (Flesch Reading Ease 60+)
- [ ] Content depth (2,000+ words for Tier 1 keywords)
