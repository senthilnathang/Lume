### Case Study Interview Guide

**Target customers:** Teams with measurable results (cost savings, time saved, feature wins)

**Interview format:** 30-min video call, 1,000+ word writeup

**Key questions:**

1. "What was your situation before Lume?"
   - Which CRM/tool were you using?
   - What problems were you having?
   - What did it cost?

2. "What changed with Lume?"
   - How did you implement it?
   - What was the learning curve?
   - How long to full adoption?

3. "What results did you see?"
   - Cost savings (quantified)
   - Time saved (quantified)
   - Features you built that weren't possible before
   - Team satisfaction improvement

4. "Why Lume specifically?"
   - What made you choose Lume vs. alternatives?
   - What would have made you leave?
   - Would you recommend? To whom?

5. "What's next?"
   - How do you see Lume growing in your org?
   - What features would help most?

**Case study format:**

- Team adoption: X%

## Part 3: Churn Prevention

### Early Warning Signals

Monitor these metrics for churn risk:

| Signal | Timeline | Action |
|--------|----------|--------|
| No login for 7 days | Day 7 | "We miss you" email + help offer |
| No new data for 14 days | Day 14 | Check-in call |
| No team activity for 21 days | Day 21 | Migration offer (switch to managed) |
| Support tickets + churn mention | Immediate | CEO outreach |

## Part 4: Community Management

### Discord Community Guidelines

**Goal:** 5,000+ members, vibrant self-support culture

**Channels to maintain:**

| Channel | Purpose | Moderation |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| #announcements | Launch updates, features | Team only |
| #general | Off-topic chat | Community mods |
| #support | Questions, troubleshooting | Fast response (team + mods) |
| #feature-requests | Feature ideas | Vote/react |
| #show-and-tell | Custom builds, workflows | Celebrate wins |
| #jobs | Hiring posts | Vetted companies |
| #introductions | New members | Welcome them |
| #dev | API, webhooks, integrations | Technical depth |

### Community Moderation Policy

**Values:** Helpful, respectful, inclusive

**Rules:**
1. Be respectful (no harassment, hate speech)
2. Search before asking (answers may exist)
3. Share knowledge (answer others' questions)
4. No spam (no unsolicited DMs, ads)
5. Use threads (keep channels organized)

**Violations:**
- First: Warning
- Second: Timeout (1 day)
- Third: Mute (7 days)
- Repeated: Ban

### Community Manager Daily Checklist

- [ ] Answer support questions (target: <2 hour response)
- [ ] Highlight great community answers
- [ ] Feature community project in #show-and-tell
- [ ] Welcome new members
- [ ] Moderate discussions (keep on-topic)
- [ ] Report abuse/spam
- [ ] Track sentiment (positive/neutral/negative)
