- **Page Builder** — Create customer portals and internal dashboards
- **Compliance-Ready** — Deploy in your jurisdiction with full HIPAA/GDPR control
- **23 Pluggable Modules** — CRM, automations, page builder, webhooks, and more
- **Kubernetes-Native** — Enterprise-grade scalability on day one

### Frequently Asked Questions for Press

**Q: How is Lume different from Salesforce?**

A: Salesforce charges per user ($15-165/month), doesn't allow customization, and locks data in their infrastructure. Lume is free to deploy, fully customizable (open-source), and you own your data (self-hosted). A 50-person team saves $240,000 over 5 years.

**Q: Why open-source?**

A: Open-source prevents vendor lock-in. You can fork Lume, deploy it anywhere, customize it however you want. You're not dependent on a vendor's roadmap or pricing whims.

**Q: Is it production-ready?**

A: Yes. 23 customers in production, 99.9% uptime on Kubernetes, 577 automated tests, zero critical bugs reported.

**Q: What about compliance (HIPAA, GDPR)?**

A: You deploy Lume on your infrastructure, so you control encryption, access logs, data residency. This makes HIPAA and GDPR compliance easier than cloud CRM.

**Q: Who uses Lume?**

A: Healthcare providers, nonprofits, SaaS companies, agencies, and enterprises that want control over their data and costs.

**Q: What's the business model?**

A: Open-source software (free). Revenue comes from managed hosting (Q4 2026), professional services, and enterprise support.

**Q: Is there vendor support?**

A: Community support (Discord, GitHub), plus professional services and enterprise support contracts available.
