## Risk Management

### Red Zone Risks (Critical)
- ✅ Infrastructure failure → Load testing, CDN, auto-scaling, rollback plan
- ✅ Email system failure → Warmup, authentication, backup provider, contingency
- ✅ GitHub compromised → Account security, backups, restoration plan

### Orange Zone Risks (High Impact)
- ✅ Poor conversion funnel → Landing page A/B testing, form optimization
- ✅ Negative press → Security audit, transparency pledge, response templates
- ✅ Team member unavailable → Backups assigned, procedures documented

### Yellow/Green Zone Risks (Medium/Low)
- ✅ Content flops → A/B testing, pivot quickly based on data
- ✅ Competitor response → Positioning documented, response templates ready
- ✅ Influencer response low → Increase frequency, test different angles
- ✅ SEO slow → Link building accelerated, content repurposing expanded

**All risks have mitigation strategies documented in `risk-assessment-contingency-plan.md`**

## Month 1 Optimization

### Daily Cycle (Every morning, 9:00 AM UTC)
1. Collect metrics (GA4, email, social, GitHub)
2. Review vs targets
3. Identify anomalies
4. Plan daily test (button color, form fields, CTA copy)

### Weekly Cycle (Every Friday)
1. Analyze week performance
2. Compare to targets
3. Identify patterns (what worked, what didn't)
4. Plan next week optimization

### Testing Framework
- Deploy 1 small change per day
- Measure impact over 48 hours
- Keep winners, iterate on losers
- Target: 15%+ improvement in conversion rate per week

**Complete framework in `month-1-optimization-framework.md`**
