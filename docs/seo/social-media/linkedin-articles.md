## Article 1: Announcement Article (1,200 words)

**Headline:** "We're Open-Sourcing Lume: Why Teams Are Ditching Salesforce in 2026"

I'm proud to announce that we're open-sourcing Lume, a modern CRM platform built for teams that want to own their data.

For two decades, Salesforce has dominated the CRM market with a simple model: charge per user, lock in customers, keep raising prices. It's worked brilliantly for them. But it's been broken for everyone else.

Today, we're offering a different path: unlimited users, zero vendor lock-in, full data ownership.

### Why We Built Lume

I spent 10 years selling SaaS software. Every sales team I worked with had the same complaint: Salesforce costs too much and doesn't fit our process.

A 20-person company pays $15,000/year. By year 5, that's $75,000+. Add Zapier integrations, training, consulting, and the real cost approaches $150,000.

Meanwhile, 80% of the features go unused. Sales teams are forced into Salesforce's workflow instead of building their own.

We knew there had to be a better way.

### The Open-Source Advantage

Open-source CRM isn't new. Odoo, ERPNext, and SuiteCRM exist. But they're complex, dated, and designed for IT departments, not users.

Lume is different. It's:

- **Modern**: Built with Vue 3, React, NestJS. UI rivals Salesforce.
- **Simple**: Deploy in 1 hour. No setup consultants needed.
- **Customizable**: Modify code, add fields, build workflows without developers.
- **Yours**: Deploy on your servers, your data stays with you.
- **Honest pricing**: No per-user fees. Infrastructure cost is transparent ($50-500/month).

### What You Get

**Visual Entity Builder**: Define your business data without code. Create custom fields, relationships, and workflows.

**30+ Automation Actions**: Trigger actions when deals move, contacts are created, payments received. No Zapier subscription needed.

**REST API + Webhooks**: Integrate with your tools. Real-time data sync. No rate limits.

**Page Builder**: Build customer portals, internal dashboards, partner sites. No HTML required.

**Compliance Ready**: Deploy in your country. Implement HIPAA, GDPR, SOC2 as needed. You have the control.

### The Economics

Let's talk money. Over 5 years, for a 50-person team:

**Salesforce**: $360,000+ (licensing, integrations, training, consulting)
**Lume**: $120,000 (infrastructure, minimal training, community support)

**Savings: $240,000** (66% reduction)

For a 100-person enterprise: Salesforce is $1.5M. Lume is $200K. That's not a 10% difference. That's a 87% difference.

### Why Startups Are Switching

Yesterday, I talked to a healthcare startup. They were spending $200K/year on Salesforce + Zapier + consulting to build a custom clinical case management system.

With Lume, they'll spend $50K/year on infrastructure and have better control.

Another founder: "Salesforce forces our entire sales process into a box. With Lume, we built the CRM around our process."

### The Data Ownership Revolution

Here's the shift happening in 2026:

**2010s:** "Cloud SaaS is the future. Give all your data to vendors."
**2020s:** "Wait... we own all this customer data. Do we trust vendors with it?"
**2026:** "Let's keep our data. We'll manage our own infrastructure."

Kubernetes normalized self-hosted infrastructure. Open-source normalized scrutiny. Lume is riding both waves.

When your customer data is on your servers, in your jurisdiction, managed by your team, something shifts:
- Security liability is clearer
- Compliance is achievable  
- Customization is infinite
- Lock-in is zero
