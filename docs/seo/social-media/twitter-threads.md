## Threading Tips

**Post timing:**
- Spread threads across 3-5 days
- Post at different UTC times to reach different zones
- Engage with replies immediately

**Engagement tactics:**
- Quote retweet with your take
- @ mention other founders/influencers
- Ask questions in replies
- Share specific metrics/numbers

**Visual assets:**
- Add 1-2 images per thread
- Screenshots of Lume UI
- Growth charts, cost comparisons
- Customer testimonials

**Call-to-action:**
- Every thread ends with: "Deploy free at lume.dev"
- Include link to relevant blog post
- Encourage "Reply with your use case"
- Build community (#LumeCRM hashtag)
